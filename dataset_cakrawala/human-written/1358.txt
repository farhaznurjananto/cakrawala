This too shall pass

Photo by Monica Gozalo on Unsplash

You who feed me fingers and toes

to play, walk, touch, dance, hug,

Let not sorrows of the past hold me back -

Help.

Let not sorrows of the present hold me back -

Help.

Let not sorrows of the future hold me back -

Help.
