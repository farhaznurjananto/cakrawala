We are excited to announce that MINGO has been listed on AlteumX Exchange and to mark the occasion we have a total of 10,000,000 XMC to give away to our users worldwide!

To show our gratitude and support to Mingo and its supporters, we at AlteumX will be depositing 500 XMC into every account with existing XMC until March 15th, 2019.

Additionally, a trading contest will be held over a period of one month in which 5,250,000 XMC will be rewarded.

TRADING COMPETITION

Competition period: 2019/02/15 00:00 AM to 2019/03/15 12:01 PM (UTC).

CONTEST A: 4,000,000.00 XMC in total to be won

Users will be ranked based on their increase trading volume (buys/sells) in XMC over the course of the competition period.

Users will be rewarded as follows:

1st: receive 1,500,000.00 XMC

2nd: receive 1,000,000.00 XMC

3rd: receive 750,000.00 XMC

4th-10th: evenly split a pool of 750,000.00 XMC

CONTEST B: 1,000,000 XMC in total to be won.

A pool of 1,000,000 XMC will be evenly split between each account with an increase of 250,000.00 XMC or more over the course of the competition period.

CONTEST C — SOCIAL MEDIA

All eligible contestants will be entered into a selection process where one random winner will be chosen and be awarded with 250,000.00 XMC. Contestants must meet all of the criteria for eligibility in order to be awarded the 250,000 XMC rewards.

Eligibility criteria consists of the following:

Retweet the contest promotional poster: https://twitter.com/Alteum_Official/status/1095473508510035968

Must be following all ALTEUM and MINGO twitter accounts: @Alteum_Official and @mingocoin

AlteumX must receive proof via screenshot submitted through this google form: https://goo.gl/forms/75eeZrxTD1vDtomB2. This must be done before the contest end date.

Note:

AlteumX reserves the right to cancel or amend the competition or competition rules at our sole discretion.

Winners in Contest A will be ineligible to win rewards in Contest B.

Rewards will be issued within 2 weeks after the competition.

The funds will be deposited to the winners’ wallet in AlteumX.
