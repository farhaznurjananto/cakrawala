“Game of Thrones” led Sunday’s cable charts, per usual, with a 3.9 rating in adults 18-49. That’s equal to its number from the previous week — and better than the combined rating of the shows ranked second through fifth on Sunday.

Among other Sunday originals, “Preacher” (0.7) and “Keeping Up with the Kardashians” (0.7) were each down a tenth of a point vs. the previous week, as was “Veep” with a 0.4. “Silicon Valley” held steady at 0.9.
