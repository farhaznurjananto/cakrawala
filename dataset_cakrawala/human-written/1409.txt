The recycling process is really important for protecting and cleaning the environment. But beside this there are hidden advantages of recycling process. Cloth recycling also delivers amazing advantages to environment and mankind. Bay state textile is eminent textile company that deals it cloth recycling process. It is connected to several municipal communities and educational institutes. They collect used clothes that are still in intact position to be worn again. Through this process, reusable clothes can be sent to the developing nations for reuse. Winter is the cruel season for the poor and patients.

There are many deaths recorded in winters. The world is full of poor and poverty-stricken people, who are in great need of food, shelter and clothes. By delivering them clothes to wear is great charity work. Moreover, total mass of landfills can be reduced to a greater extent than will save nature. Bay state textile employs numerous people, who are earning their living by working for bay state textile.
