6 big tomatillos were washed and the husks were taken off. 1-2 serrano peppers 1/2 cup onion 2 medium or 1 large avocado 1 bunch of cilantro, 1 lime's worth of juice, and salt to taste. Put everything in the Vitamix container. Start on variable speed 1, go up to 1,0, then high, and blend for about a minute, if necessary using the tamper.
