(CNN) Cleanthony Early of the New York Knicks was robbed and shot in Queens on Wednesday morning, according to police. Authorities said Early was shot once in the left leg and was taken to Elmhurst Hospital Center in stable condition. Based on preliminary information, police said it appeared Early had just left a gentleman's club around 4:30 a.m. when three vehicles surrounded the taxi he was in and a group of people -- at least one of whom was armed -- ordered him to get out of the vehicle.
