Deworm the World Initiative More than 600 million children are infected with parasitic worms, including more than 400 million affected by soil-transmitted helminths. The World Health Organization (WHO) estimates that more than 2 billion people are affected by helminth infections. Soil-transmitted helminth infections are among the most common infections worldwide and affect the poorest and most deprived communities. Deworm the World Initiative works with governments to deliver school-based deworming programs that reach hundreds of millions of school-age children and improve health and education outcomes. They are committed to ensuring that deworming programs are well-run, evidence-based, and meet the highest standards of quality, transparency, and impact.

Equality for Women in India

Equality for Women in India is a nonprofit organization that is committed to empowering women and girls in India to reach their full potential through education.

Free Wheelchair Mission Free Wheelchair Mission provides mobility to the physically impaired in developing countries, like India, through the distribution of simple, durable, rugged, all-terrain wheelchairs.

Gates Foundation The Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation works to help all people lead healthy, productive lives. In developing countries, it focuses on improving people’s access to quality education, health care, and economic opportunities. The Gates Foundation also supports global efforts to address major challenges, such as malaria, HIV/AIDS, and climate change.

Hands On India

Hands On India is a nonprofit organization that uses hands-on, experiential learning to empower students in India to become leaders in their fields.

Heifer International

Heifer International provides sustainable land and livestock improvements to low-income families in developing countries. These families are able to improve their livelihoods, reduce poverty, and build a better future for their families.

International Rescue Committee

The International Rescue Committee provides humanitarian assistance to people who are displaced by conflict and disaster. They work in more than 30 countries worldwide.

L’Oreal

L’Oreal is a global beauty company that works to improve the quality of life for women and girls around the world. They support a number of initiatives that improve the lives of women and girls in developing countries, including their partnership with the World Health Organization (WHO) to fight breast cancer.
