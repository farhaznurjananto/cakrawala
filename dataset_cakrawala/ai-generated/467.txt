Over a decade has passed since Michael Showalter and David Wain's iconic '80s camp comedy, "Wet Hot American Summer" graced our screens. Now, fans are eagerly awaiting the Netflix prequel series, "Wet Hot American Summer: First Day of Camp." In anticipation, Netflix has released new cast photos featuring Elizabeth Banks, Amy Poehler, Bradley Cooper, Paul Rudd, Christopher Meloni, and more, as they return to their Camp Firewood roots.

According to David Wain, "Wet Hot American Summer: First Day of Camp" is a prequel to the original movie. While 15 years have passed in real life, the show's characters are portrayed as being two months younger. The original movie was set on the last day of camp in the summer of 1981, while the prequel series takes place on the first day.

While images of new cast members such as John Slattery are absent from the recently released photos, the excitement for the series continues to build. "Wet Hot American Summer: First Day of Camp" is set to premiere on Netflix on July 31st. Fans can expect to see characters such as Nina Hellman as Nancy, Molly Shannon as Gail, Judah Friedlander as Ron, Joe Lo Truglio as Neil, Ken Marino as Victor, Janeane Garofalo as Beth, Elizabeth Banks as Lindsay, Christopher Meloni as Gene, Amy Poehler as Susie, Bradley Cooper as Ben, Paul Rudd as Andy, and Marguerite Moreau as Katie.
