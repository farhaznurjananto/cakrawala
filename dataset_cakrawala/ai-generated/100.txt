While it may not always seem like it, these two characters are voiced by the same actor:

1. Spongebob Squarepants
2. Patrick Star

Both Spongebob and Patrick are voiced by actor Tom Kenny. This means that both characters share the same voice actor and the same mannerisms. This is especially noticeable when the two characters are talking to each other. Because of this, it can be easy to confuse the two characters. However, once you know who the voice actor is for each character, it will be much easier to distinguish between them. Thanks for watching! :)
