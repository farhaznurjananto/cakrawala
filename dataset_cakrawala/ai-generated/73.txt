Sekeping Kong Heng, in the charming town of Penang, is a small boutique hotel located at the heart of the historical area of Penang.

The hotel is a short walk from the Penang Hill Railway Station, and is surrounded by the heritage shophouses andPre-War houses.

The hotel is also within walking distance to the Penang Peranakan Museum, and the Penang Islamic Museum.

The hotel has a total of 12 rooms, all of which are equipped with air-conditioning, a flat-screen TV, a minibar, and a private bathroom.

The hotel also offers a 24-hour front desk, a tour desk, and a luggage storage.

The hotel offers a range of breakfast options, including local and international cuisine.

Kekeping Kong Heng is perfect for guests who want to experience the historical and cultural attractions of Penang.

The hotel is also perfect for tourists who want to stay in a comfortable and friendly environment.

Kekeping Kong Heng is the perfect place to stay if you want to explore the charms of Penang!
