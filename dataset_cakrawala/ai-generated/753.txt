Another Google service liberates its users from the yoke of browse-and-select file selection. On modern, HTML5-capable browsers like Chrome and Firefox, YouTube supports drag-and-drop video uploading. It's a bit odd that the feature doesn't perhaps support Safari and Opera, both of which are fairly HTML5-friendly, but it's a nice time saver for serious YouTube users in any case.
