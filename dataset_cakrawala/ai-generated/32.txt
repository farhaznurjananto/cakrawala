GIGABYTE FM2+ Series Motherboards GIGABYTE FM2+ series motherboards feature AMD A78/ A88X chipsets and support for the latest AMD A-series and Athlon processors. The A78/ A88X chipsets provide native support for 4 USB 3.0 and 6 USB 2.0, and GIGABYTE FM2+ series motherboards offer the industry's first USB DAC-UP port - a convenient port designed to power users' USB DACs, offering a clean, noise-free power supply for users' USB DACs. GIGABYTE FM2+ motherboards also feature GIGABYTE Ultra Durable 4 Plus technology, GIGABYTE UEFI DualBIOS and GIGABYTE On/Off Charge for USB devices.
