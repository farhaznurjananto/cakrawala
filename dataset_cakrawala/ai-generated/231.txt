If you're looking to step outside of your traditional Christmas Eve or Christmas Day dinner, or if you're in need of a last-minute spot to grab a meal, the Cleveland Independents consortium of restaurants has got you covered. With over two dozen restaurants open on Christmas Eve and Christmas Day, you're sure to find something to satisfy your appetite.

On Christmas Eve, Saturday, December 24th, multiple restaurants across the Cleveland area will be open to patrons. 811 Kitchen Bar Lounge will be open from 5 PM to midnight. Blue Canyon Kitchen and Tavern in Twinsburg will be open from 4 PM to 7 PM. Bruno's Ristorante in Cleveland will be open from 1 PM to 9 PM. Cabin of Willowick will be open from 4 PM to 8 PM. Creekside Restaurant and Bar will be open from 11 AM to 4 PM. Edwin's in Shaker Heights will be open until 10 PM, and fire food and drink will offer brunch from 10 AM to 2 PM, and dinner from 5 PM to 10 PM.

Other restaurants including L'Albatros Brasserie, Luna Bakery & Cafe, Melt Bar & Grilled, Provenance at The Cleveland Museum of Art, The Woods, and Zinc Brasserie, will be open for limited hours, offering an exciting Christmas Eve dining experience.

On Christmas Day, Sunday, December 25th, two restaurants will be open. SASA will be open from 5 PM to 10 PM in Shaker Heights, and Red, the Steakhouse will offer brunch from 11:30 AM to 2:30 PM, with dinner options until the restaurant closes at 7 PM.

Remember to make sure you verify hours and any potential availability before arriving. Have a Merry Christmas!
