Innocent wish... 
 ...can turn your life upside down! 
 
 You have always struggled with  inner loneliness. When asked what you crave most, you wanted a way to overcome it. 
  How could you have even foreseen that the threads of your destiny would intertwine with those of her three unknown men who weren't even human? 
 Can you find a way to break the magical ties that hold you together?  This is a one woman project by Asch aka Mama Asch. Progress is slow but steady. Hold the line!
