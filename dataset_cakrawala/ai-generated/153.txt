Phoenix police have arrested a woman accused of stealing an ambulance from a hospital and taking it for a joyride.

Officials say the woman, whose name has not been released, stole the truck from St. Joseph's Hospital and Medical Center in Phoenix around 11 p.m. Monday.

The woman crashed the ambulance into a pole on the hospital grounds, but was not injured.

Authorities say they found the woman walking around the hospital grounds after the crash and took her into custody.

The truck's driver was not injured, but was taken to the hospital to be evaluated.

The ambulance has been returned to the hospital.

The woman is being held on suspicion of felony theft. It wasn't immediately clear if she had an attorney.
